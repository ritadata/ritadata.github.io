What does the Rita App do?

What are the key features of the Rita app

Last updated over a week ago

The Rita app consists of three main Features: View, Control, and Earn.
Below are the current list of features and capabilities of our app

View
  • Know how much is your data worth: The Rita app allows you to see and track your data value within a few minutes after download.
  • Find out who owns your data, what companies use it for and whether they are trustworthy: We provide full visibility and transparent in how your data is being used.
  • See the breakdown of ads you've clicked on and time spent on websites
Control
  • Control your data and excercise your GDPR rights: We enable you to easily excercise your GDPR rights with a push of a button by restricting companies you don't know and don't trust (we do the dirty work for you).
  • Our control feature is automated and managed using an AI which will send emails on your behalf if you wish to remove your data from specific companies
  • See the breakdown of ads you've clicked on and time spent on websites
Earn
  • Know how much is your data worth: The Rita app allows you to see and track your data value within a few minutes after download.
  • Find out who owns your data, what companies use it for and whether they are trustworthy: We provide full visibility and transparent in how your data is being used.
  • See the breakdown of ads you've clicked on and time spent on websites
