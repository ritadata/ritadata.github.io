We know your data is a very sensitive issue. That is why we prepared a list of most commonly asked questions

What Personal Data does Rita Store?

We only store your login credentials, email and password (encrypted) and the date and time you create your account. These credentials are securely stored on our cloud partner, AWS.



What anonymised data does leave the Rita App?

The only data that leaves the app is the list of companies you shared data with. We need to have the list in order to search for the company’s email in our database. This allows us support you in sending Right to be Forgotten requests to companies.



Where is my data stored?

By default, all your personal data is stored on your phone. If you decide to share your email address with us we store it on a Postgres server provided by AWS located in AWS “Euro data center hub”, Ireland.
