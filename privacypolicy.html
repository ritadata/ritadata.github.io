Privacy Policy for Rita Personal Data

At Rita Personal Data BV, accessible at Ritapersonaldata.com, our main priority is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that is collected and recorded by Rita Personal Data, how we use it and why. Our mission at Rita is to put you at the centre of control over your personal data. This is why your data is stored on your local device (mobile phone).

Protect your Privacy

The only personal data that Rita collects are your encrypted login credentials, which are stored on our secure database.

Our App will NEVER
  • Share information with third-parties without your consent
  • Identify your close contacts
  • Save personal data without your consent
  • Save or store any of your Facebook or Google login credentials
  • Perform any action on your behalf other than the actions you request in the App (Get data, Delete data, Manage ads settings)

    • You choose

    We always give you the freedom of choice:

  • Which data you collect (Google, Facebook,...)
  • Who you share your data with (feature coming soon)
  • What information you store on Rita’s secure server (feature coming soon)

    • Privacy-by-design

    Private is private. We honestly care about your privacy. Rita App is designed with maximum protection of your privacy in mind. Here is how:
  • Your personal data is stored on your device by default.
  • Independent specialists were consulted to give feedback on the design of our systems. When designing and throughout development.
  • The data you collect is encrypted.
  • Our foundational principles of Privacy by design were regularly consulted when developing the App and making architectural choices.

    • What personal data does Rita store?

    We only store your login credentials, email and password (encrypted) and the date and time you create your account. These credentials are securely stored on our cloud partner, AWS.

    Children's Information

    An important priority for us is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity. Rita Personal Data does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.

    Cookies

    In order to optimise your experience, Rita Personal Data website uses ‘cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information. We use cookies to:
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Measure effectiveness of website changes
  • For further information visit allaboutcookies.org

    • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

    Legally speaking we are the “Data Controller” of your information. This is because we have predefined how your data is processed in the app. And that makes us legally hold this status. However your personal data you collect from “sources” doesn’t leave your device without your explicit consent. If you wish your email address to be removed from our systems, please contact us. In certain circumstances, you have the following data protection rights:
  • The right to access, update or to delete the information we have on you.
  • The right of rectification.
  • The right to object.
  • The right of restriction.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to withdraw consent.
