What Payment platforms does Rita currently use?

We currently use Stripe as a payment provider. Once you have met the minimum cashout threshould you will be able to connect a Stripe account

How long do withdrawals take to process once confirmed?

Ensuring you account is fully connected, withdrawals usually take around 5-7 business day to arrive into you connected bank account Via. Stripe.

What are the restrictions to receiving payments?

Our system automatically detects duplicate accounts and restricts users from claiming multiple rewards. Payments into you account may also be paused if excessive activity of withdrawals have been made
